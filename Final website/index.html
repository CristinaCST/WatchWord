Our Vision

To tell Africa's stories through African voices. To inspire, educate, and empower audiences worldwide. To bring authentic conservation, adventure, and cultural storytelling to the global stage.

In 1963, Mutual of Omaha's Wild Kingdom brought authentic African wildlife into the homes of millions of Americans. This groundbreaking series shaped the world's understanding of wildlife conservation. More than 60 years later, while the world around it has evolved, many of the problems on the front lines of human-wildlife coexistence remain the same.

WATCHWORD has entered a unique partnership with Sustainable Wildlife Trust and Cornerstone Technologies (creators of WayPoint TV) to develop a first-of-its-kind content platform focused exclusively on stories surrounding real life African conservation.
